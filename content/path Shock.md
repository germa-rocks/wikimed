### Key points

- **[Shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) = tissue [hypoperfusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** → systemic oxygen deficit and organ dysfunction 12.
- **Four classic types:** hypovolemic, cardiogenic, obstructive, distributive — each with distinct hemodynamic profiles 1.
- **Cellular dysoxia:** impaired oxygen use → [anaerobic metabolism](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), ↑ [lactate](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), organ failure 34.
- **“Cold” vs “warm” [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#):** low‑output (hypovolemic, cardiogenic, obstructive) vs high‑output early distributive (septic/[anaphylactic](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)) 15.
- **Mixed [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** (e.g., [sepsis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) with sepsis‑induced [cardiomyopathy](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)) may show features of both low and high output states 1.

### Overview of [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) categories and hospital etiologies

Common to all forms is **circulatory failure with inadequate tissue [perfusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) and [oxygen delivery](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)**, leading to [metabolic acidosis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) and multiple‑organ dysfunction 1.

### Macro- and microcirculatory physiology

Macrohemodynamics depend on the **pressure–flow–resistance** relationship and **autoregulation**.

- **ΔP = CO × [SVR](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** describes [mean arterial pressure](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) generation 8.
- **Autoregulation** redistributes blood to maintain cerebral and coronary [perfusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) when systemic pressure falls 8.
- **Centralization of flow**: baroreceptor‑mediated [vasoconstriction](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) shunts blood from [skin](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), gut, [kidneys](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) toward [brain](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) and [heart](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) 8.
- **[Microcirculation](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#):** [capillary](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) exchange governed by **[Starling forces](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)**; [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) disturbs these pressures, promoting [interstitial](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) [edema](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) and reduced oxygen [diffusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) 8.

### Cellular dysoxia and [ischemic](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) injury

When [perfusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) or oxygen utilization fails, **[circulatory hypoxia](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** ensues 24.

- **Inadequate O₂ delivery** → switch to **[anaerobic glycolysis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)**, [lactate](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) accumulation, [acidosis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), and **[free radical](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) injury** upon reperfusion 34.
- **[Calcium](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) influx and [ATP](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) depletion** → organelle damage, enzyme leakage, [apoptosis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)/[necrosis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) 4.  
    This underlies organ dysfunction such as “[shock liver](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#),” [acute kidney injury](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), and [encephalopathy](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) 19.

### Mixed and sequential [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) states

[Shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) phenotypes may **overlap**:

- **[Sepsis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) with sepsis‑induced [myocardial](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) [depression](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** → concurrent distributive and cardiogenic components 1.
- **[Massive PE](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) with [RV failure](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** → obstructive plus cardiogenic.
- **Hemorrhagic trauma with secondary inflammatory [vasodilation](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** → hypovolemic + distributive.  
    Recognition requires **[echocardiography](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) and [hemodynamic monitoring](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** (e.g., RUSH/RACE protocols) 1.

### Clinical [correlation](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#): history clues to mechanism

Interpretation of key history features helps target etiology:

- **Weight loss, night sweats, [fever](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** → infection or [malignancy](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) → distributive/[septic shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) 510.
- **[Chest pain](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [palpitations](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [syncope](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** → cardiac [ischemia](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)/[arrhythmia](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) → cardiogenic or obstructive 17.
- **Recent trauma, [hematemesis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [melena](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** → hypovolemic/[hemorrhagic shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) 1.
- **[Dyspnea](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), pleuritic [pain](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [hemoptysis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** → [pulmonary embolism](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) → [obstructive shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) 1.
- **Drug or [allergen](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) exposure** → [anaphylactic shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) 1.
- **Spinal injury or [CNS](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) insult** → neurogenic distributive [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) 1.

### Practical implications

- Identify **[shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) type** early using [hemodynamics](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [POCUS](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), and clinical profile 1.
- **[Immediate hemodynamic support](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** (fluids, [vasopressors](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), oxygen) should proceed in parallel with etiologic diagnostics 1.
- Reassess frequently, as **[shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) states evolve dynamically**, and management must adapt to mixed or refractory physiology.
