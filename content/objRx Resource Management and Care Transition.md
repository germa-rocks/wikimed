Rx) Resource Management and Care Transition: Adaptations to stabilizing the patient outside the ideal environment (the ICU). Transfer care at the exact right moment and in a structured manner

### Key points

- **Stabilization outside [ICU](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#):** Early ABCDE, large-bore [IV access](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), monitoring, immediate fluids/[vasopressors](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)12.
- **Transfer criteria:** Persistent [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), need for invasive ventilation or [vasopressors](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), refractory [hypotension](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), mechanical support requirement134.
- **Timing:** Immediate transfer once initial stabilization achieved and definitive care resources lacking45.
- **Resource management:** Prioritize airway, [perfusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [hemodynamic monitoring](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), early specialist consultation12.
- **Structured [handover](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#):** Use standardized communication tools (e.g., [IPASS](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [SBAR](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)), minimize interruptions, ensure contingency plans67.

### Stabilizing [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) outside the [ICU](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)

Initial management should prioritize **rapid stabilization and identification of etiology** using the **ABCDE** approach and early hemodynamic support before transfer12:

- **Airway/Breathing:** Provide [supplemental oxygen](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), use [basic airway maneuvers](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#); prepare for [intubation](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) if airway protection or ventilation is required123.
- **Circulation:** Insert **≥ 2 large-bore [peripheral IVs](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)**, or intraosseous/central access if necessary12. Begin **[fluid resuscitation](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** (e.g., 500–1,000 mL NS or [LR](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) bolus over 10–30 minutes), repeating as indicated by response18.
- If **not [fluid responsive](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)**, or with refractory [hypotension](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) ([MAP](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) < 65 mm Hg), initiate **[vasopressors](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** (first-line: [norepinephrine infusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) 0.01–0.4 mcg/kg/minute)1.
- **[Hemodynamic monitoring](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#):** continuous [ECG](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [pulse oximetry](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) (unreliable if hypoperfused), blood pressure, [urine](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) output (> 0.5 mL/kg/hour); consider arterial/CV line if available1.
- **Reassess frequently** and call for critical care support early in resuscitation12.

### Resource-adapted stabilization principles

In limited-resource settings or outside the [ICU](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#):

- **Perform serial ABCDE assessments** and interventions such as fluid bolus or [vasopressor](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) [titration](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) at the bedside2.
- Use **[POCUS](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** to guide diagnosis and volume assessment when other imaging is unavailable2.
- **Avoid delays** in transfer for extensive diagnostics once life-threatening causes have been ruled out or treated45.

### Criteria for transfer to higher-level care

Transfer to an [ICU](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) or tertiary facility is indicated when any of the following are present:

- **[Refractory shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** despite fluid and [vasopressor](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) therapy1.
- **Need for invasive ventilation** or [advanced airway](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) protection13.
- **Requirement for continuous hemodynamic or [mechanical circulatory support](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** ([IABP](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [ECMO](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#))134.
- **Persistent end-organ [hypoperfusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** ([altered mental status](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [oliguria](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) < 0.5 mL/kg/hour, [lactate](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) > 2 mEq/L)1.
- **Diagnosis requiring specialized intervention** (e.g., ruptured [AAA](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), severe trauma, [refractory shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) etiologies)45.

Transfer should occur **as soon as the patient is hemodynamically stable enough for transport** and the receiving team has accepted the case45. If transfer facilities are unavailable, maintain temporizing measures with mechanical and pharmacologic support under continuous monitoring13.

### Structured transfer and [handover](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) protocols

**[Effective communication](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** between referring and receiving teams minimizes [adverse events](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#):

- **Use standardized frameworks** such as **[IPASS](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** (Illness severity, Patient summary, Action list, Situation awareness, and Synthesis) or **[SBAR](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)**67.
- **Ensure continuity:** transfer/[handoff](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) by a clinician directly involved in care and verbally confirm understanding (“[read-back](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)”)67.
- **Document clearly:** diagnoses, active issues, [hemodynamic targets](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [vasopressor](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) dosages, airway status, and contingency plans6.
- **Environment:** minimize distractions during [handoff](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), involve nursing and transport teams synchronized with [handover](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) timing6.

### Stepwise communication during transfer

**Critical components of [handover](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) for [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) patients:**

1. **Current status:** Vitals, [MAP](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) goals, [vasopressor](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)/[inotrope](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) doses, [urine](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) output.
2. **Access and monitoring:** Line locations, infusions, [mechanical ventilation](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) parameters.
3. **Therapies in progress:** [IV fluids](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [antibiotics](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [blood transfusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#).
4. **Contingencies:** What to do if [hypotension](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [arrhythmia](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), or airway compromise occurs.
5. **Destination readiness and confirmation** from receiving physician before departure457.

### Post-[ICU](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) follow-up

After stabilization and [ICU](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) discharge, use a **[critical care transition program](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** to reduce deterioration risk; patients receive continued reevaluation from critical care teams to prevent [adverse events](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) during step-down care9.
