### Key points

- **Early [vasopressor](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) initiation** recommended for persistent [hypotension](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) after or during fluids; [norepinephrine](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) first-line 123.
- **Add [vasopressin](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** if [hypotension](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) persists despite moderate [norepinephrine](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) doses 1234.
- **Peripheral [vasopressor](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) use** acceptable to avoid delay; ensure large-bore, [proximal](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) IV, frequent monitoring 135.
- **[MAP](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) target:** ≥ 65 mm Hg in adults; 60–65 mm Hg acceptable in older adults 13.
- **Transition to central access** once feasible or multiple agents required 56.

### Early initiation of [vasopressors](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)

- **Start [norepinephrine](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) promptly** when [hypotension](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) persists after adequate fluids or if [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) is severe at presentation 12.
- For **[septic shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)**, the Surviving [Sepsis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) Campaign (SSC 2026) advises [fluid resuscitation](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) with ≥ 30 mL/kg [crystalloids](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), with **[vasopressors](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) added early** (even concurrently) if unstable [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) features exist (e.g., mottling, [cyanosis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), [altered mentation](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)) 3.
- Rationale: timely [vasopressor](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) use **restores [perfusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) pressure faster** and **limits [fluid overload](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** 3.
- Persistent [hypotension](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) ([MAP](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) < 65 mm Hg) despite fluids defines need for [vasopressors](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#); **goal [MAP](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) ≥ 65 mm Hg** is supported by both AMBOSS and SSC guidelines 123.
- In **older adults (≥ 65 years)**, SSC 2026 allows a **lower initial target (60–65 mm Hg)** over higher values 3.

### [Vascular access](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) strategy

- Begin with **two large-bore [peripheral IVs](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) (≥ 16 G)**; use **intraosseous (IO)** if peripheral access fails 15.
- If **central access unavailable**, **peripheral [vasopressors](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) are acceptable** to prevent [perfusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) delay, provided the line is [proximal](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), well‑secured, and closely monitored for extravasation 123.
- Safety data show **≈ 6 % extravasation risk**, generally mild when safety protocols used (frequent site checks, immediate antidote if infiltration) 3.
- Use **[central venous catheter](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** once feasible, especially when: multiple vasoactive agents needed, high‑dose infusions ongoing, or invasive monitoring indicated 56.
- Central venous catheters remain preferred for long-term administration or when [vesicant](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) risk is high 56.

### Practical workflow

1. **Initiate [fluid resuscitation](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) immediately** ([crystalloids](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), 30 mL/kg); reassess response.
2. **Start [norepinephrine](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) early** if [MAP](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) < 65 mm Hg during or after fluids; use **peripheral route if [central line](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) pending**.
3. **Target [MAP](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) ≥ 65 mm Hg** (or 60–65 mm Hg in older adults).
4. If **persistent [hypotension](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)**, add **[vasopressin](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** (0.03–0.04 U/min).
5. If still refractory, **add [epinephrine](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)** or consider [inotrope](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) if cardiac dysfunction suspected.
6. **Transition to central access** once stable access and monitoring established.
7. Frequently reassess [perfusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)—**[urine](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) output > 0.5 mL/kg/h**, normalization of [lactate](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#), or improvement in [capillary refill](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) 1.

### Monitoring and escalation

- Continuous **cardiac and BP monitoring**, ideally via [arterial line](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) when [vasopressors](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) are used 1.
- Use **serial [lactate](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) levels** and **[capillary refill time](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) ≤ 3 s** as adjunct [perfusion](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) markers; [lactate](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) trends inform adequacy of resuscitation 123.
- If persistent [shock](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) despite optimized therapy, evaluate for **[critical illness–related corticosteroid insufficiency](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)**, **[metabolic acidosis](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#)**, or **[mechanical circulatory support](https://next.amboss.com/us/ai-mode?cid=0ffead7b-532b-46c8-8942-d55be92eaa3b#) options** 1.
